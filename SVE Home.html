How It Works

SELLERS & BUYERS

SVE provides sellers the freedom of their weekend without the need to host or coordinate showings with unserious buyers and provides in person vehicle exposure to hundreds of potential buyers every week. SVE provides buyers a safe, low pressure, and central location to view multiple vehicles in a single day.

For Sellers
Register, Drop off, Sold.
Pick your weekends.

One weekend, a whole month, or show until it sells. We show capacity in real time so you know what's filling fast.

Register your vehicle.

Fill out seller and vehicle details. We tie it to a scannable QR code on your printable window pass.

Park & sell.

Check-in 8–9 AM Saturday. Buyers scan your QR and text you directly. All vehicles off STC property by Sunday 11:59 PM.

For Buyers
Shop, Connect, Buy.
Benefit 01
Safe & verified.

Every vehicle is owner-registered with a verified phone and email. Scan the QR window pass to confirm the listing on the spot — no catfish, no parking-lot scams.

Benefit 02
One central lot.

Fifty-plus cars, trucks, bikes, and boats — all in one place at Savannah Technical College. Compare side-by-side instead of driving across town for a single viewing.

Benefit 03
Time-saving by design.

Walk the rows, scan QRs to text owners instantly. See in one afternoon what would normally take a week of appointments and back-and-forth Craigslist messages.
